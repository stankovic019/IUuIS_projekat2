HCI Project 2 by Dimitrije Stanković, PR81/2022
To add new entity, open 'Network Entities' and press CTRL + N
To delete entity, select it and press SHIFT + DELETE
To undo all changes and go back to the beginning or to the selected item in history, press CTRL + SHIFT + Z
Press CTRL + E to exit application, and perform all changes to the entities
To connect items in 'Network Display', right mouse click on first and then right mouse click on second
To also connect items in 'Network Display', press the corresponding F key for the first one, and then the corresponding F key to the second one
To remove item from box in 'Network Display' press CTRL + corresponding F key
